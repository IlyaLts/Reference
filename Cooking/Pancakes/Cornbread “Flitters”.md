## Ingredients

- Kefir
- Eggs
- Salt
- Sugar
- Baking soda
- Flour
- Oil

## Instructions

### Making the mix for Cornbread

1. Add an egg to a pot.
1. Add half of a teaspoon of salt.
1. Add three tablespoons of sugar.
1. Mix everything.
1. Add a cup of kefir or a little bit more.
1. Remix everything.
1. Add half of teaspoon of baking soda.
1. Remix everything.
1. Add a cup of flour or a little bit more. It depends on the amount of added kefir.
1. Remix everything.
1. Add two tablespoons of oil.
1. Remix everything.
1. Wait for at least 30 minutes.

### Making Cornbread "Flitters"

1. Heat a pan with oil in it.
1. Switch the stove to the middle level. *(5)*
1. Pour a tablespoon of the mix into the pan.
1. Close the lid.
1. When the bottom side is ready, flip flitters, and close the lid again.
