## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Eggs *(Two)*
- Salt
- Flour 

## Instructions

1. Clean potatoes. 
1. Grate potatoes in a bawl.
1. Add two eggs. 
1. Mix everything. 
1. Add two and a half teaspoons of salt. 
1. Add three tablespoons of flour. 
1. Remix everything. 
1. Heat a pan, then switch the stove to the middle level. *(6.5)*
1. Pour a tiny bit of oil into the pan.
1. Drop a tablespoon of the mix in the pan.
1. Flatten it.
1. Flip potato pancakes when the bottom side is ready.
