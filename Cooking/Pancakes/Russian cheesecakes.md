## Ingredients

- Cottage cheese (Pay attention that cottage cheese should have soft, very dry, and crumbly texture)
- Eggs *(Two)*
- Salt
- Sugar
- Farina
- Flour

## Instructions

### Making the main ingredient for Russian cheesecakes
1. Put cottage cheese in a pot.
1. Grind it if needed. Then, mix it.
1. Add two eggs.
1. Mix it.
1. Add half of a teaspoon of salt. Maybe a bit more if needed.
1. Add four tablespoons of sugar.
1. Mix it again.
1. Add four teaspoons of farina.
1. Mix it again.
1. Add two tablespoons of flour.
1. Mix it again.
1. Wait for at least 15 minutes.

### Making Russian cheesecakes
1. Add two tablespoons of flour to a plate.
1. Add a tablespoon of the cottage cheesecake to the flour on the plate.
1. Make a ball rolling the cottage cheesecake on the plate side to side.
1. Pour oil into a pan and switch the stove to the maximum level. *(9)*
1. When the pan is heated, switch the stove to the middle level. *(6)*
1. Take a cheesecake ball and flatten it, then put it on the pan.
1. Flip the cottage cheesecakes when the bottom side is ready.
