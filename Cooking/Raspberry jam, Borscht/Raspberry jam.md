## Ingredients

- Raspberry
- Sugar

## Instructions

1. Grind raspberry.
1. Add one cup of sugar per one cup of raspberry in a pot.
1. Mix it.
1. Switch the stove to the middle level. *(6)* *(Slow warming)*
1. Once the raspberry jam is boiling *(You should see small bubbles or something)*, turn off the stove immediately.
1. Wait for the raspberry jam to cool down and mix it if needed.
1. Pour the raspberry jam into bottles.
