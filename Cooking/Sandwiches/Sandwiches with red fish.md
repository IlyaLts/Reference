## Ingredients

- Fish (Pink Salmon, Salmon, Chum Salmon)
Note: Salmon ("Gorbusha") is probably the best choice.
- Salt
- Bread/loaf
- Butter

## Instructions

1. Wash a fish.
1. Cut the fish in half.
1. Remove bones.
1. Salt to taste.
1. Wrap the dish with foil and paper.
1. Put it in a fridge for at least 24 hours.
