## Ingredients

- Eggs *(Three)*
- Salt
- Baking soda
- Sugar
- Flour
- Milk
- Oil

## Instructions

### Making the mix for Blini

1. Add three eggs to a pot.
1. Add one teaspoon of salt.
1. Add half of teaspoon of baking soda.
1. Add about two tablespoons of sugar.
1. Mix everything.
1. Add a little bit more than half a cup of milk.
1. Mix everything.
1. Add ten tablespoons of flour.
1. Mix everything.
1. Add about two teaspoons of oil or a little bit more.
1. Add 75% of a cup of milk or a little bit more. **(Should be liquid as it is easier to form a circle on a pan)**
1. Leave it for an hour before cooking.

### Making Blini

1. Pour a tiny bit of oil into a pan.
1. Heat the pan, then switch the stove to the middle level. *(7)*
1. Pour the mix into the pan tilting the pan from side to side while pouring, forming a circle.
1. Flip the blini when the bottom side seems ready. **(Do not wait too much time, as the blini would be dry.)**
