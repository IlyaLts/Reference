## Ingredients

- Bread or loaf
- Salt
- 2 Eggs
- Milk

## Instructions

1. Add two eggs to a bowl.
1. Mix them using a fork.
1. Pour about a half of a cup of milk in the bowl.
1. Salte to taste. *(about a half of a teaspoon or so)*
1. Mix it again.
1. Cut a loaf/bread.
1. Pour oil into a pan.
1. Heat the pan, and then switch the stove to the middle level. *(6)*
1. Dunk a slice of bread/loaf in the mix, and then put it on the pan.
1. Flip toasts when ready.
